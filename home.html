Tsunami Studios is a multinational company specializing in the planning, development and distribution of video games and the research, development and distribution of software, data and video media. The company was originally founded in 1996 as a computer graphics producer. On January 16, 2002, the company launched a game development division and re-established itself as primarily a video game company. On September 30, 2003, Tsunami Studios became a wholly owned subsidiary of MIS, in turn a subsidiary of MINET.
